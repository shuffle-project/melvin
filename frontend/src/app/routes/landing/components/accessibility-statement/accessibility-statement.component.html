Accessibility Statement

Scope of application

This accessibility statement refers to the current version of the State Act on Equal Opportunities for Persons with Disabilities (Landes-Behindertengleichstellungsgesetz - L-BGG) of December 17, 2014. The SHUFFLE project is committed to making this website accessible in accordance with Section 10 (1) of the State Disability Equality Act (L-BGG).

This accessibility statement applies to this website (www.melvin.shuffle-projekt.de). This statement explicitly does not refer to the other websites and mobile apps of the Hochschule der Medien or the universities involved in the project and not to the content of third-party providers referred to via external links.

Compliance status

This website is partially compliant with Section 10 (1) L-BGG due to the the following non-compliances.

Non-accessible content

The Melvin application for creating videos and correcting subtitles is not accessible to screen reader users or people who only use a keyboard.

We are working on the accessible implementation of the content mentioned above.

The homepage and the Melvin video player are accessible.

Preparation of this accessibility statement

This statement was prepared on 28.02.2025. It is based on a self-assessment that was conducted on 28.02.2025. This declaration was last reviewed on 28.02.2025.

Enforcement procedure

To ensure that this website meets the requirements described in Section 10 (1) L-BGG, you can contact the SHUFFLE project partners (melvin@shuffle-projekt.de) by email and provide appropriate feedback.

If the Melvin project team does not respond to your request within four weeks, you may file a complaint with the National Center for Accessibility (LZ-BARR).
