Accessible videos - recording, subtitling and sharing with Melvin

  • Open-Source
  • Free of charge
  • Work in progress

Recording

Start your recording directly in the browser, without any additional software. Plus, you have complete control over which audio and video sources you want to use.

You already have a recording? Simply upload it and use it too.

Subtitling

After recording, your videos are automatically transcribed and subtitles are created. Using the editor, you can then make the final corrections manually, either alone or together with others.

Sharing

Your recording can now be shared with our accessible player. You can simply share the link or embed the player in your website.

Of course, you can also download your files and upload them elsewhere.

Melvin is currently still under development. However, you can already test the application now.

In addition to improvements in accessibility and user experience, we are currently working on a livestream mode. Stay tuned and feel free to check back regularly.
