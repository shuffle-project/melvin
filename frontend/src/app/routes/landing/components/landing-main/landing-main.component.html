Recording

Start your recording directly in the browser, without any additional software. Plus, you have complete control over which audio and video sources you want to use.

Subtitling

After recording, your videos are automatically transcribed and subtitles are created. Using the editor, you can then make the final corrections manually, either alone or together with others.
